Electrical Surveys

Ensuring Safety and Compliance

We provide comprehensive electrical survey services, including installation reviews, periodic testing, and detailed inspections to ensure your systems are safe, efficient, and fully compliant with all UK standards.

Our work is carried out by NICEIC certified electricians who use the latest diagnostic equipment to deliver precise results, from initial installation handover to routine safety checks.

New Installations Review

Periodic Testing & Inspection

Scheduled testing to identify potential issues and ensure ongoing safety and compliance.

Certification & Handover

Safety Compliance Audits

Ensuring your electrical systems adhere to the latest health and safety standards.

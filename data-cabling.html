Data Cabling Solutions

What is Data Cabling?

Structured data cabling is the backbone of your entire IT network. It's a comprehensive system of cabling and associated hardware that provides the critical infrastructure for your business's data, voice, and multimedia traffic.

A properly designed and installed system by NSS ensures reliable connectivity, scalability for future growth, and optimized performance for all your network-dependent operations.

Our Data Cabling Services Include

Cat5e, Cat6, Cat6a Installations

Installation of copper-based networks for reliable data and PoE applications.

Network Design & Consultation

Bespoke network design to meet your specific performance and budget requirements.

Cabinet & Patch Panel Tidy

Organizing and optimizing your network cabinets for easier management and troubleshooting.

Testing & Certification

Full testing and certification of new and existing networks to ensure performance standards.
